Rocket Core is a Rocket League and League of Legends Tournament and Esports Organisation that fosters an enjoyable environment for all its staff, members and everyone involved.

ROCKET LEAGUE

To be prepared to play in our Tournaments and Events We have build an advanced scrim system for our Rocket League community. Our seeders do their best job to look at your teams' past Tournaments performance and give you a rank. In that rank you can request and accept scrims to play a Best Off Series against similar ranked teams.

LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

For our League of Legends community, we offer a highly accurate tier list for players eager to climb in Solo Q, based on intel from a large variety of partnerships and external sources, such as ProGuides Challenger League of Legends Guides, Skill Capped Challenger LoL Guides, Mobalytics and more.
